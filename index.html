A Home Away From Home

Casa Kalinaw is a newly-built luxurious and modern yet cozy and relaxing beachfront vacation home situated at the heart of San Juan, Batangas, Philippines. Located in a remote spot away from the usual crowded beaches, this private beachfront home boasts Instagrammable interiors and picturesque ocean views.

Perfect for family bonding or group getaways, our vacation home can accommodate up to 25 guests across 5 beautifully designed bedrooms. Each room features air-conditioning and private T&B for your comfort. Wake up to stunning sunrise views as the beach house faces East, offering a magical start to your day.
